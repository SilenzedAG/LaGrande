About Us

A brief description of our company.

LaGrande Automative

LaGrande Automative is a premium and authorized reseller for McLaren Automative.

The name LaGrande is made up of "La", which means "The" in french, and "Grande" which is "Great" in french and also inspired from the well known pop-singer, Ariana Grande.

Our company offers a wide range of McLaren cars. From exclusive and exotic cars that have gone out of production, to second hand cars that we have bought in from past McLaren owners.
